üéØ Victory Value Calculation

Each victory is worth different amounts based on multiple factors:

1. Opponent Quality (1-10 scale)
  • Elite teams (8-10): High value
  • Good teams (6-8): Solid value
  • Average teams (4-6): Moderate value
  • Weak teams (1-4): Low value
  • FCS teams: Fixed 0.5 (minimal)
2. Location Multiplier
  • Home wins: 1.0√ó (baseline)
  • Neutral site wins: 1.15√ó (+15%)
  • Road wins: 1.3√ó (+30%)
  • Exception: No location bonus vs FCS
3. Margin of Victory Bonus
  • 0-7 points: Linear scaling
  • 7-14 points: Diminishing returns
  • 14-21 points: Slower growth
  • 21+ points: Minimal extra benefit
  • FCS games: Max 0.2 bonus regardless of score

  • Tier 1 rivalries: +1.0 points

  • Conference context: Extra points for P4 vs P4
  • G5 beating P4: Major bonus
